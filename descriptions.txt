# Enemies
phyrexian_mite: The PHYREXIAN MITE is a small but deadly creature from the plane of Phyrexia. It has a metallic carapace that covers its entire body and a set of sharp mandibles that it uses to bite its prey. The mite is extremely agile and can move quickly across any surface, making it difficult to catch or avoid. It is a carrier of Phyrexian oil, which it uses to infect other creatures and spread the Phyrexian corruption.
abyss_watcher: The ABYSS WATCHERS, the legendary warriors of Farron's Undead Legion, are the stuff of nightmares. They are the ultimate bros, always ready to party, but once they put on their fancy red cloaks and unsheathe their fiery swords, they transform into a fearsome pack of frat boys on a rampage. They fight with the precision of synchronized swimmers, and their coordination is so tight, you'll think you're watching a dance routine. And when they start summoning their spectral buddies to the dance floor, you'll know it's time to get serious.
soldier: The Notorious SOLDIER OF GODRICK, a boss shrouded in the mystic realm of Elden Ring, stands as the ultimate challenge for any brave adventurer. With unyielding strength and an arsenal of punishing attacks, this legendary foe embodies the true essence of difficulty, leaving players with a choice - conquer the impossible or be forever trapped in the cycle of defeat.
kikimora: Introducing the KIKIMORA, a mischievous creature of folklore with glinting eyes and a wicked sense of humor. Beware, for encountering this creature may result in never being able to find your keys again, as it thrives on mischief and enjoys playing pranks on unsuspecting souls.
samodiva: A SAMODIVA gracefully dances under the golden sunlight. Her ethereal beauty enchants all who gaze upon her, yet a mischievous glint in her eyes holds a hint of trepidation. Legends whisper of her bewitching allure and the dire consequences of crossing her path, for those who dare may find themselves forever lost in a labyrinth of bewilderment, never to find their way again.
tzitzi: The TZITZI-YA-KU, known for its fabulous fashion sense, loves to strike a pose and steal the spotlight. With its dazzling and flashy demeanor, it flaunts its shimmering, shiny scales in vibrant violet and blue tones, leaving all onlookers in awe of its fabulousness. Beware, though, as its fashion-forward moves may blind you with sheer style!
tech_priest: The Tech-Priest, a formidable adversary from the depths of the Adeptus Mechanicus, is a fusion of flesh and machine. These enigmatic beings, driven by an insatiable love for technology and the Omnissiah, have undergone a metamorphosis where the boundaries between man and machine blur. Their bodies are adorned with intricate cybernetic enhancements, their mechanical appendages seamlessly integrated with their organic form. With every movement, a symphony of whirring gears and electronic hums accompanies their presence, a testament to their devotion to the divine mysteries of technology.

# Special Attacks
earworm: EARWORM - You start humming a catchy tune and infects the enemy with an earworm, causing them to become distracted and unable to focus. The enemy takes damage as their mind is consumed by the incessant melody.
fireball_barrage: FIREBALL BARRAGE - Summon a rain of fireballs from the sky, because why not make it rain fire?
flame_tsunami: FLAME TSUNAMI - Summon a massive wave of fire that will engulf everything in its path, because sometimes subtlety is overrated
gladiators_gambit: GLADIATOR'S GAMBIT - The gladiator intentionally loses the first few rounds of the fight to lull the opponent into a false sense of security, then delivers a surprise attack when they least expect it.
rick_roll: RICK ROLL - this attack is a powerful and devastating technique that can completely disorient and distract enemies. Beware, though - this attack is not for the faint of heart, as you'll need to have a certain level of swag and style to pull it off properly.
spin_to_win: SPIN TO WIN - Turn you panic-stricken daze into a deadly weapon
